    My Skills

    ML Research and Data Analysis

    In my work on predicting mutation effects with Protein-Language Model based deep learning models, I have gained proficiency in PyTorch and in different deep learning building blocks such as Transformer decoders, CNNs and Graph Nets to model protein structure. I learned Data Analysis by my cancer research projects where I used R for survival analysis and plotting, as well as SciPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Pandas etc. for statistical analysis and visualisation

    Clinical Research

    I have gained somewhat expertise on the treatment of cancer patients, targeted therapy in lung cancer as well as tumor genomics through my clinical and preclinical cancer research.

    Preclinical Research

    During my 2 months internship in the Brägelmann Lab at the Department of Translational Genomics of the University Cologne I established a platform for ROS1 mutation testing where i gained wet lab skills such as qPCR, Gibbson Cloning, Site-directed Mutagenesis, transformation in E. coli, cell culture, virus production, Western Blot and several other assays
